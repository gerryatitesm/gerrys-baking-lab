## Ingredients

| Ingredient                        | Quantity |
|-----------------------------------|----------|
| Unsalted butter (room temperature)| 1 cup    |
| **Dark brown sugar**              | 1 cup    |
| **White sugar**                   | 1/2 cup  |
| Large eggs                        | 2        |
| Extra egg yolk                    | 1        |
| Pure vanilla extract              | 2 tsp    |
| All-purpose flour                 | 2¼ cups  |
| Baking soda                       | 1 tsp    |
| Fine sea salt                     | 1 tsp    |
| Flaky sea salt (for topping)      | 1/2 tsp  |
| Dark chocolate chips (60% cacao)  | 2 cups   |

> **Intervention:** sugar ratio adjusted to a dark-brown/white blend (follow-up to session 2026-06-05).
> **Control batch:** 100% dark brown sugar recipe from session 2026-06-05.

## Procedure

1. Preheated oven to 375°F / 190°C. Lined two baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Creamed butter and sugars together until light and fluffy (~3 min).
3. Added 2 whole eggs, 1 extra yolk, and vanilla extract. Whisked vigorously for 90 seconds.
4. Folded in flour, baking soda, and fine sea salt until just combined.
5. Folded in dark chocolate chips.
6. Rested dough at room temperature for 30 minutes.
7. Portioned into 2-tablespoon rounds spaced 5 cm apart. Topped each with a pinch of flaky salt.
8. Baked 11 minutes. Edges golden; centers should appear slightly underdone.
9. Cooled on baking sheet 5 minutes, then wire rack 20 minutes before evaluation.
10. Prepared and baked the control batch in parallel using the same oven, sheet setup, and bake time.
